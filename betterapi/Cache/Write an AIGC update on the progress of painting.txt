#Slide: 3
#Header: Overview of AIGC Painting Project
#Content: The AIGC's painting project involves repainting the interiors and exteriors of its facilities. The project aims to improve aesthetics and create a conducive environment for staff and visitors.

#Slide: 4
#Header: Current Progress Update
#Content: We have made significant progress and completed painting the exteriors. We are halfway through painting the interiors and believe that we will achieve our target in three months.

#Slide: 5
#Header: Challenges and Opportunities
#Content: The project faced challenges such as determining the right palette, finding skilled painters, and meeting deadlines amidst COVID-19 restrictions. However, we see opportunities in creating jobs for local painters and boosting local economies.

#Slide: 6
#Header: Conclusion 
#Content: The AIGC Painting Project is progressing and will soon transform our facilities. We appreciate the team's dedication and hard work and are committed to delivering the project as planned.
