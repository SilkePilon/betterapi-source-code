#Header: INTRODUCTION TO NATURAL LANGUAGE PROCESSING
#Content: Natural language processing (NLP) is a subfield of artificial intelligence that focuses on the interaction between human language and computers. 

#Slide: 3
#Header: TOKENIZATION
#Content: Tokenization is the process of breaking up the text into individual words or phrases. 

#Slide: 4
#Header: PARTS OF SPEECH TAGGING
#Content: Parts of speech tagging is the process of identifying and labeling the parts of speech of words in a sentence. 

#Slide: 5
#Header: NAMED ENTITY RECOGNITION
#Content: Named entity recognition is the process of identifying and classifying named entities in text into predefined categories, such as person names, organization names, and location names. 

#Slide: 6
#Header: SENTIMENT ANALYSIS
#Content: Sentiment analysis is the process of identifying and extracting opinions, sentiments, and emotions expressed in text. 

#Slide: 7
#Header: TOPIC MODELING
#Content: Topic modeling is the process of automatically identifying topics present in text documents and grouping them together based on their similarity. 

#Slide: 8
#Header: TEXT CLASSIFICATION
#Content: Text classification is the process of classifying documents or pieces of text into predefined categories.

#Slide: 9
#Header: CONCLUSION
#Content: Natural language processing has a wide range of applications, from chatbots and virtual assistants to sentiment analysis and topic modeling. It is a rapidly growing field with a lot of potential in the future.
