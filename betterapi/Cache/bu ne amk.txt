#Header: Definition of "Bu ne amk"
#Content: "Bu ne amk" can be translated to English as "What the f*** is this?" or "What the hell is this?" It is an exclamation of surprise, confusion, or frustration.

#Header: Context of "Bu ne amk"
#Content: "Bu ne amk" is often used in informal settings, such as among friends or on social media. It can be used to express one's reaction to a variety of things, including images, videos, news, or situations.
